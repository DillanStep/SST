# Expansion Overview

SST integrates with DayZ Expansion features when the relevant modules are installed.

Two main integration areas:

- Expansion Market: trade logging and market editing
- Expansion Vehicles: vehicle purchase tracking, key generation, vehicle management

## Related mod scripts

- Market hooks: [docs/mod/scripts/SST_ExpansionMarketModule.md](../../mod/scripts/SST_ExpansionMarketModule.md)
- Vehicle purchase hook: [docs/mod/scripts/SST_ExpansionVehicleSpawn.md](../../mod/scripts/SST_ExpansionVehicleSpawn.md)
- Vehicle tracker: [docs/mod/scripts/SST_VehicleTracker.md](../../mod/scripts/SST_VehicleTracker.md)
